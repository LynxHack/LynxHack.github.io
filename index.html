About Me

I am a masters student at the University of British Columbia in Computer Engineering. Originally a mechatronics engineering student, I started off developing firmware for robots as a member of the UBC Thunderbots. Overtime, I became more and more interested in various topics in software development such as machine learning and working different kinds of learning models such as RNN and SVM.


Being a open-source developer as a hobby, I have strong knowledge with both front and back-end development and have seen apps from creation to full deployment.
